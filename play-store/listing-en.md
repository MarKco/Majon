## Full description (max 4000 characters)

```
Majon is a row counter built for knitters: one big button to count, pattern instructions that pop up by themselves exactly when you need them, and progress percentages always in sight.

COUNT WITHOUT THINKING
• Tap + after each finished row, with a confirming vibration
• Tap − to undo accidental taps
• "Go to row" if you ever lose count
• The screen stays on while you knit

INSTRUCTIONS AT THE RIGHT MOMENT
Enter your pattern's instructions as notes: "decrease by 2 at row 19", "increase at the edges from row 20 to 30", "odd rows in stockinette". Majon shows them highlighted right when you are about to knit that row, with a preview of the next row. No more pencil marks or forgotten decreases.

• Notes on a single row or a range of rows
• Notes for odd or even rows only, combinable with a range
• Edit and delete anytime

PROJECTS AND PARTS
• Unlimited projects, each with its own icon and color
• Split every project into parts: front, back, sleeves, collar
• Completion percentage per part and per project
• Yarn and needle fields to remember what you used

YOUR DATA STAYS YOURS
• No permissions, no internet, no ads
• Export everything to a JSON file and import it back anytime
• Perfect for switching phones without losing a stitch

AND MORE
• English and Italian, switchable in-app
• Light, dark or automatic theme
• Material You dynamic colors (Android 12+)
• A warm, clean design made for knitting and crochet

Whether you're knitting an intricate sweater or your very first scarf, Majon keeps the count for you — you just keep on knitting.
```
